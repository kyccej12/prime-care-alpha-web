Prime Care Alpha is thrilled and deeply
honored to express our hearthfelt
gratitude to the esteemed province of
Cebu and Governor Gwen Garcia for
entrusting us as your partner in
delivering state-of-the-art medical
assistance and care to the people of
Cebu. Our mission is to be a partner in the
development of a healthy nation and
contribute to the betterment of our
society.

As we move ahead, we promise to honor
the trust you've placed in us, forging a
legacy of health, hope, and healing that
will resonate for generations. Cebu
Province's dedication is a beacon of
inspiration, and we're grateful for this
meaningful partnership because here at
Prime Care Alpha, WE CARE.
